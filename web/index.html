Moubot
Mourinho Slack Bot who whines in your Slack channel before and after every Chelsea FC match.

Deploy

Easy deployment to Heroku. You will need: Slack team and a dedicated channel, Slack incoming web hook token, Football-data.org token.

One-click deployment coming soon !!
